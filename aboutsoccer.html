Soccer has 4 main positions.

  • Striker
  • Mid fielder
  • Defender
  • Goalkeeper

Striker: person who specialises in skills and leg power who tries to score
Mid fielder: a striker which focuses more on passing and creating chances to score
Defender: defends the goal WITHOUT using hands
Goalkeeper: the last line of defense, can use his hands
